Who's Your District?

Who's Your District displays demographic and representative
information by districts highlighted by an Esri map of Colorado. This
allows for citizens to easily identify their representative and view
breakdowns of age, sex, and race in all Colorado districts.

Data from US Census Bureau ACS 2011 113th CD Dataset
(http://www.census.gov/acs/www/data_documentation/documentation_main/)
and Esri 113th Congressional District API
(http://esriurl.com/hack4co).

Code available at https://github.com/mbarrenecheajr/whosyourdistrict.
