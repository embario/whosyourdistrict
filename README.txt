Who's Your District?

Who's Your District displays demographic and representative information by districts highlighted by an Esri map of Colorado. This allows for citizens to easily identify their representative and view breakdowns of age, sex, and race in all Colorado districts.

Data from US Census Bureau ACS 2011 113th CD Dataset (http://www.census.gov/acs/www/data_documentation/documentation_main/) and Esri 113th Congressional District API (http://esriurl.com/hack4co).

Code available at https://github.com/mbarrenecheajr/whosyourdistrict.

Built by Andy (http://www.andysayler.com), Mazin (http://www.twitter.com/mhakeem), and Mario (http://www.mbarrenecheajr.com) for Hack4Colorado (http://www.hack4colorado.com).
